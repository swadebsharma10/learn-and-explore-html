Flooding caused by incessant rain in Cox's Bazar and Bandarban for the last week have killed at least 30 people.Villagers found floating bodies of the children, who were holding each other's hands even after death, at Ferasingapara under Uzantia union around 6:30am, said Toffazzal Karim, chairman of the union, our staff correspondent reports.

The deceased were Tauhida Begum, 10, daughter of Nurul Alam of Ferasingapara, her brother Amir Hossain, 5, and their cousin Humaiara Begum, 8, daughter of Saber Ahmed. They went missing yesterday evening when they went to catch fish in the flood water, said Mohammad Omar Haidar, officer-in-charge of Pekua Police Station
